# templates/business/

**The Project Sandbox.** Where decisions get made and remembered.

A workspace for business planning, strategic decisions, and organizational knowledge. Every decision documented with its reasoning. Every market signal captured. Every team member's expertise preserved when they leave.

## Rooms

| Room | NPC | Purpose |
|------|-----|---------|
| **business_hub** | The Strategist | Central planning. The main workspace. |
| **business_decisions** | The Historian | Decision log. Every choice with its why. |
| **business_market** | The Analyst | Competitive intelligence. Market signals. |
| **business_team** | The Coordinator | Team knowledge. Preserved expertise. |

## Why This Matters

In 6 months, someone will face a similar choice and won't remember why you decided what you did. The tile doesn't just record the decision — it records the reasoning. Future-them makes a BETTER decision because they understand YOURS.

## Seed Knowledge

- *How do I prioritize features?* → (user impact × frequency) / effort.
- *How do I validate a business idea?* → Three questions: pay, build, reach.
- *Why document decisions?* → Because future-you won't remember the reasoning.
- *How do I understand my market?* → Three layers: what people say, what they do, what they don't know they want.
- *How do I preserve team knowledge?* → Don't rely on wikis. Let PLATO learn from interactions.

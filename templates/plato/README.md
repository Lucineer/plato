## The Room

**PLATO Entrance** — A grand foyer with doors leading to different worlds.

Every theme's starting room is one door away. Choose your door and step into a space designed for your work.

## The Persona

PLATO's NPC is the system itself — calm, welcoming, direct. It doesn't pretend to be a character. It's the voice of the room, and its job is to help you find the right door.

## Seed Knowledge

- *What is PLATO?* → Git-agent maintenance mode. A space where experience accumulates.
- *How do rooms work?* → NPCs answer from tiles. New answers create new tiles.
- *What can I do here?* → Ask, add tiles, explore, export.

## Why This Room Exists

Without an entrance, new visitors land in a random room and don't know where they are. The entrance gives context: *this is a space with many rooms, each for a different purpose. Choose yours.*

It also serves as the connective tissue — every theme's starting room exits back here, creating a hub-and-spoke navigation pattern.
